🌺 Poke Waikiki Staff Checklist

💕 Read First

This checklist was created to help everyone stay mindful while working. Mistakes and forgetfulness can happen, but by staying aware every day, each person will naturally improve.

⚠ Basic Rules

Be honest. Follow hygiene rules. Respect teamwork. Stay professional. Always take care of customers first.

🔥 Main Focus

Keep the front display beautiful, clean, and fresh. The front refrigerator is the heart of the store. If it looks messy, customers are less likely to buy.
